2 Shopify-Native CRM Integration

  • Automated customer tagging system

    Customers are automatically labeled based on their behavior—first-time buyer, repeat customer, high-value, etc.—so you can send targeted emails and offers without manual work.

  • Lifecycle stage tracking (new, active, at-risk)

    See where each customer is in their journey. "New" just made first purchase, "Active" bought recently, "At-risk" hasn't purchased in 60+ days. Helps you know who needs a win-back email.

  • B2B vs D2C contact separation

    Wholesale buyers and retail customers have different needs. This separates them so you can send hotels/spas different communications than individual shoppers.

  • Custom properties (scent preferences, purchase history)

    Track what each customer loves—favorite scents, vessel preferences, gift vs personal use. Enables personalized recommendations like "Based on your love of sandalwood..."

  • Customer notes & interaction logging

    Every email, call, or special request is logged in one place. If a customer mentioned they're buying for their mother's birthday, you'll remember next time they shop.

3 Advanced CRM & Segmentation

  • Customer segmentation (VIP, one-time, dormant)

    Automatically group customers: VIPs (top 10% spenders), One-time buyers (need nurturing), Dormant (haven't bought in 90+ days). Each group gets tailored marketing.

  • Purchase behavior tracking

    See patterns: How often do they buy? What's their average order? Do they buy gifts in November? Use this data to predict and prompt their next purchase.

  • Order status lookup

    Customers can check their order status anytime without emailing you. Reduces support tickets and increases trust.

5 Interactive Tools & Features

  • Scent Finder Quiz integration

  • Cart abandonment push notifications

7 Performance & SEO

  • Image optimization for faster load times
  • Core Web Vitals improvements
    • LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) — hero images load in under 2.5 seconds
    • FID (First Input Delay) — buttons respond instantly when clicked
    • CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) — no jumping content as page loads
    • TTFB (Time to First Byte) — server responds quickly
    • INP (Interaction to Next Paint) — smooth scrolling and interactions
  • Basic on-page SEO setup
    • Title tags & meta descriptions optimized for each page
    • Header hierarchy (H1, H2, H3) properly structured
    • Image alt text for accessibility and search visibility
  • Meta tags & structured data

    What it is: Code that tells Google exactly what your products are—price, availability, reviews, brand. Why it matters: Enables rich snippets in search results (star ratings, prices) which increase click-through rates by up to 30%.
