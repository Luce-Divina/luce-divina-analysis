# ADR-001: Three-Month Growth Projections for Luce Divina

**Date:** 2026-01-14
**Status:** Accepted
**Context:** New business launch projections for luxury candle brand

---

## Decision

Based on SEMrush competitive analysis and industry benchmarks, we've established realistic 3-month projections for Luce Divina's market entry.

---

## Context

Luce Divina is a new luxury candle brand with:
- **AOV:** $500
- **Launch Status:** Pre-launch (zero existing traffic)
- **Target Market:** Luxury home fragrance

We needed to answer: **What should we realistically expect in the first 3 months?**

---

| **FORVR Mood** | 873 | 10,300 | $6,100 | $95 | $6M (Yr 1) |

**Key Insight:** Even Big Ass Luxuries at $5M revenue only has 7,300 organic visits/month. Willie C Candles (emerging brand) has essentially zero organic traffic.

---

## Traffic Source Projections

### Month 1-3: 90% Paid / 10% Organic
### Month 6: 82% Paid / 18% Organic

This split is validated by SEMrush data showing emerging brands have near-zero organic traffic until brand recognition builds.

## Case Studies

### Big Ass Luxuries
- **Source:** [Shark Tank Season 16](https://sharktankrecap.com/big-ass-luxuries-update-shark-tank-season-16/)
- **AOV:** $179-284
- **Revenue:** Year 1: $1.3M → Year 3: $5M
- **ROAS at scale:** 4.5x
- **CAC:** $50
- **Profit Margin:** 20%
- **Key Success Factor:** Product differentiation ("America's Largest Candle")

### Willie C Candles
- **Source:** [willieccandles.com](https://www.willieccandles.com/)
- **AOV:** $42-109
- **Stage:** Emerging (building from zero)
- **Organic Traffic:** 1/month (SEMrush)
- **Strategy:** Art-forward positioning, community building

### FORVR Mood
- **Source:** SEMrush + industry reports
- **AOV:** $95
- **Revenue:** $6M in Year 1
- **Key Success Factor:** Founder had 3.5M YouTube subscribers (Jackie Aina)

---

## Honest Limitations

1. **No $500 candle case study exists** — Big Ass Luxuries at $179-284 is the closest
2. **Conversion rates are estimates** — Need actual ad data to validate
3. **CPC is industry benchmark** — Actual CPC may vary based on targeting

---

## Recommendations

1. **Start at $2,000-3,000/month** ad spend to gather data
2. **Expect break-even ROAS** (1.17x-1.75x) in months 1-3
3. **Month 4+ is when scaling begins** — reviews, retargeting pools, and algorithm learning compound
4. **Track actual CPC and CR** to refine projections
